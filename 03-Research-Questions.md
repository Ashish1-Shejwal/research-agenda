# Research Questions

## Overview

The following research questions define the long-term intellectual direction of my research program.

Rather than representing isolated academic interests, these questions form an interconnected research agenda focused on enabling organizations and society to adopt Artificial Intelligence responsibly, effectively, and at scale.

The questions are organized into three levels:

- Grand Challenges (10–20 years)
- Strategic Research Themes (3–10 years)
- Immediate Research Projects (1–3 years)

This structure ensures that every publication, framework, software tool, and collaborative project contributes toward a coherent long-term vision.

---

# Level I — Grand Research Challenges (10–20 Year Vision)

These are the fundamental questions I hope to spend my career answering.

## Grand Challenge 1

How can organizations adopt Artificial Intelligence responsibly while maximizing innovation, competitiveness, and long-term societal value?

---

## Grand Challenge 2

What governance models best ensure that AI systems remain trustworthy, transparent, accountable, and aligned with human values throughout their lifecycle?

---

## Grand Challenge 3

How can governments, industries, and academic institutions collaborate to establish globally applicable AI governance frameworks while accommodating regional, cultural, and regulatory differences?

---

## Grand Challenge 4

How can organizations objectively measure AI readiness before implementing Artificial Intelligence at scale?

---

## Grand Challenge 5

What factors determine long-term public trust in AI-powered systems?

---

## Grand Challenge 6

How should Responsible AI principles evolve alongside rapidly advancing foundation models, autonomous agents, and future AI technologies?

---

## Grand Challenge 7

How can Artificial Intelligence improve quality of life while minimizing unintended societal, organizational, ethical, and economic consequences?

# Long-Term Research Goals

Through this research agenda, I aim to contribute to the development of practical knowledge that enables governments, organizations, researchers, and society to adopt Artificial Intelligence responsibly.

My objective is to create research that extends beyond academic publications by producing tools, frameworks, standards, and methodologies that support real-world AI implementation.

Every research question included in this document contributes toward a single overarching mission:

> **To enable trustworthy, responsible, and human-centered adoption of Artificial Intelligence that creates sustainable value for organizations and society while preserving transparency, accountability, fairness, and public trust.**

This document will evolve continuously as Artificial Intelligence advances and new societal, technological, and governance challenges emerge.
