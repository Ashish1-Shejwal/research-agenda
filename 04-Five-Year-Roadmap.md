# Five-Year Research Roadmap (2026–2031)

## Overview

This roadmap outlines my long-term strategy for developing into an internationally recognized researcher and practitioner in Trustworthy AI Adoption, Responsible AI, Enterprise AI, and AI Governance.

The roadmap emphasizes continuous learning, interdisciplinary research, practical implementation, open knowledge sharing, and meaningful societal impact.

Rather than measuring success solely by publications, this roadmap focuses on creating frameworks, software, research, educational resources, and collaborations that accelerate the responsible adoption of Artificial Intelligence worldwide.
