# Core Research Areas

## Overview

My research focuses on enabling the responsible, trustworthy, and effective adoption of Artificial Intelligence across organizations and society.

Rather than concentrating on AI model development alone, my work examines how organizations can successfully design, implement, govern, evaluate, and scale AI systems that are aligned with human values, organizational objectives, and public interest.

My research is interdisciplinary, integrating Artificial Intelligence, management, governance, public policy, organizational behavior, technology strategy, ethics, and digital transformation.

The following research areas collectively define my long-term research agenda.

---

# 1. Trustworthy AI Adoption

## Research Objective

To understand how organizations can successfully adopt Artificial Intelligence while maintaining trust, transparency, accountability, and long-term societal value.

## Key Topics

- Organizational AI adoption
- Human trust in AI
- AI implementation challenges
- AI readiness
- Responsible deployment
- AI acceptance
- Human-AI collaboration

## Example Research Questions

- Why do organizations fail to adopt AI successfully?
- What factors influence trust in AI-enabled decision making?
- How can organizations measure AI readiness?
- Which organizational capabilities lead to successful AI transformation?

# Long-Term Research Philosophy

Although these research areas cover multiple disciplines, they are connected by a common purpose:

**To enable organizations and society to adopt Artificial Intelligence responsibly, effectively, and at scale while preserving trust, accountability, transparency, and human well-being.**

Every publication, project, framework, software tool, and collaboration I undertake will contribute toward this overarching mission.

---

# Research Evolution

Artificial Intelligence is evolving rapidly, and my research agenda will continue to adapt accordingly.

While specific research questions may change over time, the guiding principles of my work will remain constant:

- Human-centered innovation
- Evidence-based decision making
- Responsible AI adoption
- Interdisciplinary collaboration
- Practical impact
- Open knowledge sharing
- Global societal benefit

These principles will guide my contributions to academia, industry, government, and the broader AI ecosystem throughout my career.
